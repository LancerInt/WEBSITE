    Biostimulants Portfolio

    Products that enhance plant resilience, nutrient uptake, and growth performance.

    IGreen

    IGreen is a modular microbial consortium built to unlock soil nutrients, defend against stress, and drive resilient growth. Four targeted blends address core agronomic challenges.

    View Product

    Zenita

    Zenita harnesses water-soluble neem limonoids and proteins to deliver foliar nutrition and plant growth promotion with excellent compatibility in fertigation systems.

    View Product

    Cropsia

    Cropsia combines moringa and Adhatoda vasica extracts to revitalise crops under abiotic stress, supporting greener canopies and improved yields.

    View Product

    Enrhize

    Enrhize supplies high-density Glomus spp. propagules that colonise roots rapidly, improving nutrient uptake and resilience in challenging soils.

    View Product

    Orgocare

    Orgocare is a plant-extract blend designed to mitigate stress responses and maintain metabolic balance during crop transitions.

    View Product

    Blooma

    Blooma utilises premium seaweed extracts to boost flowering, fruit set, and abiotic stress tolerance in high-value crops.

    View Product

    Envicta

    Envicta combines humic, fulvic, and amino acids to restore soil structure and enhance nutrient availability in organic systems.

    View Product
